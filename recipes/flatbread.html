Flatbread

Flatbread: it's bread but in two dimensions

Ingredients

  • 500g strong white flour
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 7g sachet fast-action yeast
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 300ml water

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour, salt, yeast and oil in a large bowl and add enough water to make a soft, but not sloppy, dough
  2. Knead well. Put into a lightly oiled bowl to rise for 1 hr until doubled in size
  3. Divide the dough into 8 pieces, about 250g each, and use a rolling pin to flatten each one into a circle, 15cm in diameter, about 1cm thick.Then leave the pieces on a lightly floured baking tray to prove for 5 mins
  4. Heat a large frying pan to a medium heat and dry-fry each piece until browned on both sides, about 5 mins in total. Set aside to cool slightly before serving.
